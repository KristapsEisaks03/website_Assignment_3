HTC

HTC Vive is a popular company that has the most focus on the VR industry and they create things such as vr headsets, controllers, trackers, base stations and etc...
It has the most influence on the VR hardware and is most loved by consumers when it comes with reliability.


HTC Vive Headset

HTC Vive was the greatest headset to have as it had in its time of launch best vr experience and good controllers to play vr games with and having the best and user friendlies base station setup which costed £799.
£200 more than the rift headset but did offer premium experience at its time.

Most people would go for this headset at 2021 due to its current cheap price as there are now more headset that offer better performance and experience for VR, so price you may see about £200 or £300 or even cheaper if you are lucky.

HTC Vive Pro Headset

Vive Pro is an upgrade from the original Vive headset. It comes with built in headphones which provided much better quality of sound and uses newest 2.0 Base stations which come with improvement in FOV and with higher accuracy.

Comfort has also been upgraded so overall it is a high budget and premium headset but issue that is with this headset is the upgrade comparing to old HTC vive are little and looking by the price of around £800 or even £1000 and looking by upgrades and the price it is more worth it of buying old HTC Vive (Unless you want 2.0 Base Stations).

HTC Vive Cosmos Headset

HTC Vive Cosmos is the first headset from HTC that does not uses external lighthouses but uses inside out tracking meaning every movement and location is tracking by the headset itself using cameras on the outside of the headset.
But controllers that are with Cosmos are quite bulky and also are tracked using light which is not ideal meaning you would have lower precision of tracking comparing to Oculus. At launch it had issues with tracking in way that you could not play games normally which destroyed the experience but after a year they have completely fixed the issue but it has no way of tracking your hands behind as there is no AI that does that for you like in Oculus.
Price of the headset is around £699 which is quite expensive.
